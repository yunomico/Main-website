What We Do

Yunomi! Co. is an organization that aims to inspire individuals to partake in creative endeavours by establishing an effective network of talent. Each work group is made in such a way so that every member's skills are sharpened to the fullest in a helpful yet professional environment.

Why Join

You will learn as you work on a project! Understanding what you need to know to make your Project successful will only motivate you to learn more.

Yunomi! Co. presents an unique opportunity for its members to acquire new skills by interacting with other talented individuals. By putting an emphasis on hand on learning, Yunomi! Co. fosters a work setting where members can rapidly pick up new skills.


You only need to help out for 3-4 hours a week to get a credit! A credit is where you will be recognised on the website for your hard work!

The projects are designed to accommodate for the busy schedules of individuals working & studying full time. Thus, flexibility is top priority with a minimum time requirement of 3-4 hours.


This will build your resume and network! We plan to do joint projects with other clubs, easy projects which mean less time working and more experience to write on your resume!


It’s fun! We don’t pester your team to finish up your Project, so have fun making new friends in your new club!
